Title: Reflections on My Computer Science Degree Journey at RYK Science College

Date: 29th April 2026, 10-11 p.m., Wednesday

Author: Arnaud Jitendra Rathod

Dear Journal,

Tonight, I find myself reflecting on my journey in the computer science course at RYK Science College. Tomorrow marks the final exam of my 6th semester and the culmination of my entire degree - a momentous occasion that will conclude my academic life and leave only the task of collecting my mark sheet and degree certificate remaining.

I joined the college in June 2023, following a disappointing 12th examination result which left me at RYK Science College despite my aspirations to attend a better institution. Initially, I felt out of place amidst the students and faculty, as if I did not belong there. My experience was further complicated by an absence of individuals who aligned with the kind of people I wanted to surround myself with.

Throughout these three years, I have struggled internally with doubts about my capabilities and the validity of my place in the course. I encountered teachers who I found difficult to connect with, while others like Tilak ma'am in maths and Geeta ma'am in electronics inspired me and provided guidance that has proven beneficial. Among my peers, there were only a handful who exhibited ambition and aspirations, such as Aditya, Arsh, and Sushant.

In my second year, I encountered Ahire ma'am, the strictest practical teacher I have ever had. Her teaching style was challenging at times, but her approach helped me develop discipline in my coding practices. Today, I realize that she may have been onto something when she spoke of the positive impact her teaching had on her students' lives.

This past semester, I secured an internship during which I juggled both work and college responsibilities. Despite the pressure, I managed to prepare for exams with limited resources, relying primarily on textbooks and online resources. The experience was grueling, but it has instilled a sense of satisfaction in me as I approach the finish line.

Reflecting on my time at RYK Science College, I find myself feeling a mix of emotions - satisfaction for completing the journey, bitterness towards some experiences, and gratitude for the opportunities that came my way. Despite the challenges, I am grateful to have met decent people who have made this experience somewhat bearable.

Tomorrow marks the end of an era as I walk into the examination hall for the final time. I feel a strange sense of relief and sadness knowing that it's almost over. But I am glad that I am still here, having completed the journey in one piece. Here's to the next chapter in my life!

Yours sincerely,
Arnav Jitendra Rathod
Bachelor of Science (Computer Science)
